SWGZ

A mini program for Guangzhou Architecture Expo visitors

Open-source on GitHub

A WeChat mini program designed for visitors of a Guangzhou architecture exhibition. It helps users explore the cultural background of local landmark buildings and provides interactive AI features.

GitHub

Key Features

  • Illustration-style map: visually presents featured buildings and their locations.
  • AI image recognition: take a photo to identify a building and view details.
  • AI Q&A: ask questions about Guangzhou architecture on demand.
  • AI poster: generate a personalized commemorative poster.
